What can you do to make money ?

Running

During running, the tissues are saturated with oxygen, which is a great help to blood circulation. Thanks to this cardio exercise, the heart muscles gain strength and endurance, the vascular walls are strengthened, and their tone increases.

Walking

Regular exercise strengthens muscles, bones, and joints, reducing the risk of any injuries, including fractures. Walking improves your mood, helping you cope with depression and anxiety. Walking at a good pace develops your heart and lungs. Proven: The more people walk, the lower the mortality rate - at any age

Cycling

Cycling increases your energy expenditure - your metabolism speeds up and your oxygen supply to organs and tissues, including your muscles and brain improves; Increased endurance. Regular cycling increases your general endurance and makes you more alert and stronger.
